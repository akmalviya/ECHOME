Powerful Features

EchoMe offers a unique way to connect with your future self through time-delayed messaging

Send Future Messages

Compose messages to be delivered to yourself at a specified future date, creating a unique way to reflect on your past thoughts and emotions.

Custom Delivery Dates

Choose the exact date and time when you want your message to be delivered, allowing for personalized and timely communication with your future self.

Receive Past Messages

Experience the surprise and nostalgia of receiving messages that you wrote to yourself in the past, creating a powerful connection across time.

Secure & Private

Your messages are encrypted and stored securely until their delivery date. We prioritize your privacy and data security above all else.

How EchoMe Works

Send a message to your future self in just a few simple steps

1

Write a Message

Compose a message to your future self. Pour out your thoughts, dreams, and aspirations.

2

Choose Delivery Date

Select the date in the future when you want to receive your message. It could be weeks, months, or even years ahead.

3

Confirm and Send

Review your message, make any final edits, and then send it off to be delivered to your future self.

4

Receive Via Mail

Find your message in your inbox on the specified date. Open it and reconnect with your past self.
